font-weight: bold;	font-size: 16pt;">The Free Software Foundation 
(FSF) is a nonprofit with a worldwide mission to promote computer user 
freedom. We defend the rights of all software users. <a href="https://www.fsf.org/about/"><span style="text-decoration:underline">Read more.</span></a>
  </p>

<p></p> 


<p><strong>LibrePlanet is an annual conference hosted by the Free Software Foundation for people who care about their digital freedoms. <a href="https://my.fsf.org/civicrm/event/info?reset=1&amp;id=71">Register for the 2018 conference today. </a></strong> See: <a href="https://libreplanet.org/2018/speakers">LibrePlanet.org for photo credits</a>.</p>

      <div style="margin-left: 530px">
        <p style="float: left;">Free software developers guarantee
 everyone equal rights to their programs; any user can study the source 
code, modify it, and share the program. By contrast, most software 
carries fine print that denies users these basic rights, leaving them 
susceptible to the whims of its owners and vulnerable to 
surveillance.</p>
        
        <ul style="float: left;">
          <li>The FSF provides critical infrastructure and funding
 for the <a href="https://gnu.org">GNU Project</a>, the 
foundation of the popular GNU/Linux family of free operating systems and
 the keystone of the Internet.</li>
          <li>Our <a href="https://www.fsf.org/campaigns">Campaigns Team</a> creates
 educational materials about free software, convenes the yearly <a href="https://libreplanet.org/conference">LibrePlanet 
conference</a> and goes toe to toe against powerful interests that
 threaten computer user rights.</li>
          <li>Our <a href="https://www.fsf.org/licensing">Licensing & Compliance 
Lab</a> defends freely licensed software from proprietary 
hoarding, advises on licensing issues, and certifies devices that <a href="https://www.fsf.org/ryf">Respect Your 
Freedom</a>.</li>
        </ul>
        <p style="float: left;"><strong>With your support, 
we've done these things for more than 30 years. Help sustain us for many more; <a href="https://fsf.org/jfb">please become a member 
today</a>.</strong></p>
